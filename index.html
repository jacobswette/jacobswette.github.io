Introduction

About

I am an MPH Epidemiology student at the University of South Florida College of Public Health, concurrently completing a Graduate Certificate in Applied Biostatistics. My research centers on vector-borne disease ecology, the impact of adverse childhood experiences on chronic health outcomes, and One Health approaches that bridge human, animal, and environmental health.

My academic journey is interdisciplinary — spanning health sciences, public health, philosophy, Chinese Medicine, and Biomimicry — giving me a unique lens for understanding complex health challenges facing human and non-human species. I am a healer, researcher, environmental & health philospoher focused on how ecological and social systems shape health.
